How Much Snow?

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four snowflakes below. By clicking on each snowflake you will add a specific amount of snow to your total amount.

You win the game by matching your total amount of now to the random ammount of snow, you lose the game if your total amount goes above the random amount.

The value of each snowflake in hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each snowflake
